The game is a dungeon crawler where the player can only move north or east after clearing the room. The player can return to the last room if and only if they are still in the door frame. The rooms get harder the further the player goes; while the rooms to the north get harder faster. The player moves with w, a, s, d to move up, left, down, and right. The player can swap equipment with the q button. To use the left, up, right, and down keys to use the equipment in that direction. Press escape to open the pause menu to stop the game and use the power ups. Press q, w, e, r to upgrade Hp, Attack,Defense, and Mp. To win get to the  last room at the top right and clear that room. The .bin file is needed to save scores and load them. After the game is over the player will need to reopen the jar to play again.
